Steps

STEP 1

Upload Your Image

Select or drop the files using the 'Upload File' button. It can be a scanned/non-scanned image file. Once uploaded the software would take a few seconds to process the file. After getting processed, move forward to the next step.

STEP 2

Edit & Review

Once the document is processed, the software would take you to the review screen. In the review screen, you can see the extracted text at the left panel of your screen. If you find an issue with the extracted data, you can correct and fix it right there.
